Online Safety

  • Don't share personal info. Names, addresses, phone numbers and passwords should remain private.
  • Use strong passwords. Use unique passwords and a password manager when possible.
  • Beware of suspicious links. Only click links you trust; double-check URLs.
  • Keep software updated. Updates patch security vulnerabilities.
  • Report and block harmful content. If you see something illegal or dangerous, report it to the platform and local authorities if required.
